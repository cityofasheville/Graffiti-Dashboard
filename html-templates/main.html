The City of Asheville is cleaning up graffiti vandalism.

Tough problems like ridding our city of graffiti vandalism can only be successful if the whole community works together. The goal of the 90-day intensive cleanup program is to help property owners rid their properties of graffiti at a minimal cost. After September 30, 2014, property owners will be responsible for 100% of the cost of cleanup.
